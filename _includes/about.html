About

We are an independent traditional egalitarian minyan in Berkeley, California. We aim to host one Kabbalat Shabbat and one Saturday morning davening per month. We plan to experiment with different models of davening and learning. We hope to create a vibrant, energetic, and inclusive community committed to halakha.
